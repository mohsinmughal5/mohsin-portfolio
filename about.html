About Me

I am Ch. Mohsin Khan — a Teacher, Mentor, Counsellor, Trainer, and Leader with 18 years of experience in Teaching, Training, Human Development, Student Counselling, and Leading Teams.

My passion lies in Leadership and Organizational Behavior, focusing on the human role in development and the psychological stimuli that influence behavior and performance.

I’m currently pursuing my PhD in Management Sciences (HR specialization) at Air University, Islamabad. My research explores how motivation, perception, and learning drive organizational effectiveness.

Beyond my leadership in education, I also work as a part-time Web Developer, building clean, responsive, and purposeful websites that align with learning and communication goals.
